About The Event

Join us for a comprehensive one-day summit focused on cloud security best practices, featuring industry experts and hands-on workshops. The Cloud Security Summit will provide insights into the latest threats, compliance requirements, and security solutions for cloud environments.

Whether you're a security professional, cloud architect, or IT manager, this summit will equip you with the knowledge and tools to enhance your organization's cloud security posture.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the registration fee?

Your registration includes full access to all sessions, workshops, networking events, breakfast, lunch, and refreshments throughout the day, as well as all conference materials.

Is there a dress code for the event?

Business casual attire is recommended for all attendees.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, all sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees after the event.

Is there parking available at the venue?

Yes, complimentary parking is available at the Tech Hub Conference Center garage.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Refunds are available up to 14 days before the event. After that, you can transfer your registration to a colleague.
