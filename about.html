About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the story behind Cloud User Group

Our Mission

Cloud User Group (CUG) is dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of cloud computing enthusiasts, professionals, and innovators. Our mission is to:

  • Provide a platform for knowledge sharing and learning about cloud technologies
  • Connect professionals across different industries who are interested in cloud computing
  • Promote best practices in cloud adoption, migration, and management
  • Support career development through networking and mentorship opportunities
  • Bridge the gap between academic learning and industry application

Our Vision & Values

We envision a future where cloud computing knowledge is accessible to all, empowering organizations and individuals to leverage these technologies for innovation and growth.

Community

We believe in the power of community and collective knowledge sharing.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and encourage innovative thinking.

Inclusivity

We welcome members from all backgrounds, experience levels, and industries.

Education

We are committed to continuous learning and skill development.

Our History

2018

The Beginning

Cloud User Group was founded by a small group of cloud enthusiasts who wanted to create a community for knowledge sharing and networking.

2019

First Conference

CUG hosted its first annual cloud computing conference, bringing together over at 100 professionals and students.

2020

Virtual Transition

Adapted to global challenges by transforming all events to virtual formats, expanding our reach to international participants.

2021

Mentorship Program

Launched a formal mentorship program connecting experienced cloud professionals with those starting their journey.

2022

Regional Chapters

Expanded to include regional chapters across the country, creating more localized communities.

2023

Industry Partnerships

Established strategic partnerships with leading cloud service providers and tech companies.

Today

Growing Community

CUG continues to grow, with thousands of members across different regions and industries, united by their interest in cloud computing.
